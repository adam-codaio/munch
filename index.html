Needfinding

We interviewed 7 people ranging from an expert crowd researcher to an extreme member of the crowd. We learned about a diversity of perspectives, eventually narrowing our focus to reducing surplus and waste by leveraging crowd power.

Slides

POV and Experience Prototypes

We conducted a second round of focused interviews in order to formulate Point of Views for our potential users. We then created and tested 3 experience prototypes in order to more clearly define our product.

Concept Video

We developed user tasks and then storyboarded and filmed a concept video for our product. Alex Tran starred as a hopeless romantic in a modern-day love story.

Low-fi Prototype and Testing

We created numerous design sketches for diverse implementations of our product and then built a low-fi prototype of our best design. We then performed a series of simple usability tests leveraging PoP software in order to gain feedback on our interface and workflow.
